Song: I Got A Boy

Artist: SNSD

Album: Dancing Queen

Released: January 1, 2013

Songwriter(s): Lee Soo Man (executive), Yoo Young-jin, Steve Booker

I Got a Boy (stylized as I GOT A BOY) is the fourth Korean studio album (sixth overall) by South Korean girl group Girls' Generation. It was released for digital download on January 1, 2013 by S.M. Entertainment, and was made available for physical sales the following day by KT Music. Musically, the album is characterized as combining elements from a wide range of genres including R&B, new wave, and EDM. This is the last studio album and Korean studio album with their founding member Jessica before her dismissal of the group in 2014 and subsequent departure from SM Entertainment in 2015 respectively.
