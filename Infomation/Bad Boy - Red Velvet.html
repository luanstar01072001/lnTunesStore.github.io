Song: Bad Boy

Artist: Red Velvet

Album: The Perfect Red Velvet

Released: January 29, 2018

Songwriter(s): JQ, Moon Hee-yeon

"Bad Boy" is a song recorded by South Korean girl group Red Velvet, serve as the single from The Perfect Red Velvet, a reissue of their second studio album Perfect Velvet. The hip hop-influenced R&B and trap song was produced by production team The Stereotypes, Maxx Song, Whitney Phillips and Yoo Young-jin, while the Korean lyrics were adapted by lyricists JQ and Moon Hee-yeon.
