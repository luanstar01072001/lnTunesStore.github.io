Song: As If It's Your Last

Artist: Black Pink

Album: As If It's Your Last

Released: June 22, 2017

Songwriter(s): Teddy Park, Brother, SuChoice 37, Future Bounce, Lydia Paek

"As If It's Your Last" (Korean: 마지막처럼; RR: Majimagcheoleom) is a song recorded by South Korean girl group Blackpink, released on June 22, 2017 by YG Entertainment. It was written by Teddy Park, Brother Su, Choice37, Future Bounce, Lydia Paek and produced by Teddy Park. Musically, it has been described as a synth-pop, classical, house, reggae and moombahton song, whose lyrics talk about finding and losing love. Commercially, the song debuted atop the Billboard World Digital Songs chart, at number two on K-pop Hot 100 and at number 3 on the Gaon Digital Chart. The single sold more than 2.5 million copies in South Korea.
