Song: Feel Special

Artist: Twice

Album: Feel Special

Released: September 23,2019

Songwriter(s): J.Y.Park

Feel Special is the eighth extended play by the South Korean girl group Twice. It was released by JYP Entertainment on September 23, 2019, and features the title track of the same name as well as a Korean version of their Japanese single "Breakthrough". It follows their April 2019 EP Fancy You. Consisting of seven tracks featuring genres from EDM, hip hop and 90s music, all members of Twice participated in the album as songwriters. Music critics complimented the album production, with the EP being a commercial success for the group recording sales of over 400,000 copies. It was Twice's best-selling album until the release of their succeeding EP, More & More.
