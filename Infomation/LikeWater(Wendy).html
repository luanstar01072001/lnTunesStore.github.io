Song: Like Water

Artist: Wendy

Album: Like Water

Released: April 5, 2021

Songwriter(s): Kenzie and Yoo Young-jin

Like Water is the debut extended play by South Korean singer Wendy. The EP was released on April 5, 2021, by SM Entertainment and consists of five tracks, including the two title tracks, "When This Rain Stops" and "Like Water". The EP makes Wendy the first Red Velvet member to make a solo debut. News of the EP was first reported on March 10 by South Korean entertainment outlet StarNews.
